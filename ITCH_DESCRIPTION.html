Made for Gamedevjs Jam 2026

Rush Order is a fast-paced factory game where you manually operate a conveyor belt pipeline. Pick up items, feed them into assembly stations, package the results, and deliver them to waiting cars — before the line backs up and everything goes wrong.

Gameplay

Items spawn on the belt at a steady pace. Drag them into the correct assembly stations, wait for them to assemble, then move the finished product through packaging and into a delivery car. Every successful delivery earns points. Every item you drop or let burn costs you. The screen will let you know when things are going badly.

Controls

Click & Drag Pick up and place items
Release Drop item onto a station, packager, or car
