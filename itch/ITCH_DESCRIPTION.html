Made for Gamedevjs Jam 2026

Rush Order is a fast-paced factory game where you manually operate a machines. Pick up items, feed them into assembly stations, package the results, and deliver them to waiting cars — keep it clean and make the boss happy.

The factory doesn't slow down for you. As time passes, the belt speeds up, assembly takes less time, and orders grow more demanding. Keep up, or get fired.

Gameplay

Items spawn on the belt at a steady pace. Drag them into the correct assembly stations, wait for them to assemble, then move the finished product through packaging and into a delivery car. Every successful delivery earns points. Every item you drop or gets destroyed costs you. The screen will let you know when things are going badly.

Controls

Click & Drag Pick up and place items
Release Drop item onto a station, packager, or car
